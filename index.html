At Synechron

Impact

Synechron is where I’ve led and contributed to high-impact design initiatives across UX, research, and systems design that supports large-scale project rollouts. I’ve been working on some incredible initiatives (the perks of fintech, can’t share much).

However I can say that my work is now at more scale, in terms of the projects I am designing, scale of userbase, number of teams, team members, depth and specificy of the solutions and so on.

While working

I am fortunate to have some amazing leadership that has inspired me to understand more about the emotional quotient that is needed to be a good leader.

Also amazing teammates who are a breeze to collaborate with and are great friends. Makes going to office on Mondays a bit easier, exciting even 😉

Before joining

I was a UX designer who had just started off and stepping into a team of veteran designers (most of whom were working out of US) was the most amazing experience of my career. I had a ton of things to learn and the team was super amazing and accommodating.

At H&R Block

Impact

H&R block was already a UX mature organisation and as a UX designer, I could integrate some third party solutions, create designs for new features, new tax rules and create designs for A/B testing by evaluating metrics from the product.

My role helped me learn core concepts of product, creating design solutions & multidisciplinary collaboration.

I was able to contribute to the design force of H&R block, adhere to the standards of their design culture & create some designs that returned some positive metrics.

While working

Collaborated with the super amazing content team, where I learned the importance of UX writing & worked with a few members of the product team, where we would get into the depth of the problem. Working at H&R Block with a specialised design team and their powerful design system would shape me for the future of my career!

At Speridian

Impact

I was able to deliver design solutions for Easyhealth, a health tech portal based out of Hong Kong, worked on O360, Speridian's HRM solution & as well as many other tasks that supported the sales team and generated revenue for us.

While working

I had a super amazing design team with whom I shared my learnings, stayed in close connection even though we were fully remote and as well as learning from a multi talented team about design, development and teamwork.

Before joining

Speridian was my first corporate experience. I had the experience of working on concepts. When I joined the team my fresh perspective was welcomed & there I learned that all designers, junior to senior are an asset to the team!

At Payfuture

Impact

As the only UX designer hire, I built a robust design function in the organisation.

Payfuture currently has a proper product design function, which the product team can utilise, the dev team can take advantage of for faster & efficient production and the business can leverage for better organisation.

I was able to create design systems and a proper positioning for design function at Payfuture & advocate design to the organisation.

While working

I worked closely with the product team, to create solution and establish a strategic method for development, help QA evaluate with more detailed design standards checklist & create design stylesheets for various projects for the changes and scaling to lean back on.
